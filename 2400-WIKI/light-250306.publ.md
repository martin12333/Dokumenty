# Philips Hue Smart Plug

what is the philips hue smart plug?

The Philips Hue Smart Plug is a smart plug that allows you to control your lights from anywhere in the world. You can turn your lights on and off, set schedules, and even control them with your voice using Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, or Apple HomeKit. The smart plug is easy to set up and works with any standard electrical outlet.

The Philips Hue Smart Plug is a great way to add smart lighting to your home without having to replace your existing light fixtures. You can use the smart plug to control your lights remotely, set schedules, and even create scenes that change the color and brightness of your lights. The smart plug is compatible with all Philips Hue products, so you can easily integrate it into your existing smart lighting system.
